Welcome to the CellDOM

Welcome to the petri dish. Become the dominant organism by using your cells to infect other cells. Every cell is unique, and uses different strategies to infect others.

Avatar Movement

Your cells are the green #'s. Don't lose all of them or you'll lose the game. Your cells follow your @ sign (moved with WASD keys) and respond to special commands (keys are shown in the in-game ui).

Gameplay Mechanics

The CellDOM world is a tile based grid. Every turn, cells decide on a tile to move to. If a cell moves into a tile that another cell occupies, it will infect that cell and override its behavior.

Gameplay Mechanics

CellDOM is populated with a variety of cells that vary in ferocity. Some cells will work together to swarm others while other cells act like parasites.
